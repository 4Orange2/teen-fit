Structured exercise refers more to organized sports, workouts, and timed runs. Teens who want to improve their physical strength should definitely consider a little structured exercise.
As we believe that giving you more freedom is the best option, we don’t have pre-defined workouts, because that’s not proven to work well for teens. We suggest using our analytics page in order measure various components of your physical activity (e.g. your conditioning/runs)

You can set goals regarding your exercise and what you want to improve!

Welcome to TeenFit! A website that can be used by teens to help them gain their daily physical activity in a fun, routine way. With free analytics systems to analyze various aspects of your physical activity, advice to keep with long-term exercise habits, and teen-geared exercise ideas, this website is one that teens around the world have long been waiting for.

  Why Teens?
 Teens have long wanted a software aimed to help them, so they aren’t forced to use adult-based platforms
Starting early can help prevent problems in later life
Teens are at that age where they’re able to start gaining some independence; they’re able to work with these apps effectively
Suicide rates and mental health disorders are high among teens, physical activity can act as a prevention mechanism
